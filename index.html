Programs

B.COM.(HONS)

Program Code: N3

Commerce serves as the bridge between society and the ever-evolving business landscape. Considering rapid technological progress, the B.Com Programme within our Department/School is designed to cultivate a student cohort equipped to navigate the complexities of modern business.By offering a comprehensive curriculum, the programme empowers students to explore diverse avenues in employment and entrepreneurship, fostering self-reliance along the way. The introduction of Skills Enhancement Courses (SEC) further enriches the learning experience, enabling students to acquire and apply new skills for societal development. Drawing from disciplines such as Accounting, Finance, Human Resource Development, Marketing, International Business, Law, and Taxation, the programme encompasses a broad spectrum of knowledge. Additionally, through Generic Electives, Skill Enhancement, Value Added, and Ability Enhancement courses, students have the opportunity to engage with subjects beyond the realm of Commerce, promoting interdisciplinary learning and holistic growth.
